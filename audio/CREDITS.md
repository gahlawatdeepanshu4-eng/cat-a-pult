# Audio credits

The creature hit sounds in `audio/` are short trimmed clips of real recordings
from **Wikimedia Commons**. Each is used under the license shown. Clips were cut
to ~0.3–0.9s, loudness-normalised and re-encoded to MP3; no other changes.
